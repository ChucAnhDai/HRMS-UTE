Actors & Roles

Definition of system users and their permissions.

Administrator

System Owner

Has full access to all system modules and configuration settings.

  • Manage all user accounts & roles
  • Configure system settings (Taxes, Holidays)
  • Manage Departments & Positions
  • View all Payroll & Financial data

Manager

Department Head

Responsible for managing employees within their specific department.

  • Approve/Reject Leave Requests
  • Approve/Reject Overtime Requests
  • Evaluate Employee Performance
  • View Department Attendance Logs

Employee

Standard User

Regular staff members with access to personal data and self-service features.

  • View Personal Profile & Contracts
  • Check-in / Check-out (Attendance)
  • Submit Leave & Overtime Requests
  • View & Download Payslips

Candidate

External

Applicants who have applied for open positions.

  • Submit Application (CV)
